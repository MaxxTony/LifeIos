Account Deletion Request

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your habits, tasks, and AI coach history will be permanently deleted.

How to delete your account:

1. Open the LifeOS Prime app on your device.

2. Go to Settings > Edit Profile.

3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.

4. Confirm the deletion.

Submit a manual request:

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us from your registered email address.

Email Support to Delete Account

Please allow up to 7 business days for manual deletion requests to be processed.
