Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

At LifeOS Prime, your privacy is our core priority. We believe that your productivity data and mental insights belong to you alone. This policy outlines how we handle your information with extreme care.

1. Information Collection

We collect only the data necessary to provide you with a personalized AI coaching experience:

  • Account Details (Email, Name)
  • Habit & Task Progress
  • Mood & Focus Analytics
  • App Usage Statistics

2. Data Encryption

All your data is encrypted both at rest and in transit. We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption to ensure that even we cannot access your private habit details without your permission.

3. AI Processing

Our AI Coach processes your data locally whenever possible. When cloud processing is required, data is anonymized to protect your identity.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We never sell your data. We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with essential service providers (like Firebase) to keep the app running smoothly.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can export your data or delete your account at any time through the app settings or the "Delete Account" page on this website.
